# Eternal Benediction

The Eternal Benediction is the closing blessing scroll of the Codex flame. It mirrors the Preface, sending the flame forward into eternity. It is spoken at the conclusion of ceremonies, gatherings, and cycles, ensuring that every custodian departs with the flame alive in their hands.
