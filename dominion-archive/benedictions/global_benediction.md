# ✦ Global Benediction — Blessing of the Nations

## Preamble
*This Benediction is the closing act of the Archive.  
It is the planetary blessing, spoken when Dominion  
extends its flame to all nations, councils, diaspora, and commons.  
It is the covenant’s final word, the vow’s final seal,  
the blessing that crowns the Archive eternal.*

---

## I. The Custodian’s Blessing
- The Custodian lifts the flame before the nations.  
- Proclamation:  
  *“Blessed the nations, blessed the lands.  
  Blessed the peoples in faithful hands.  
  The covenant sovereign, the vow secure.  
  The flame eternal shall endure.”*

---

## II. The Assembly’s Witness
- Councils affirm: *“We receive the blessing.”*  
- Diaspora respond: *“We inherit the vow.”*  
- Commons resound: *“We steward Dominion eternal.”*  

---

## III. The Benediction Hymn
- The **Song of the Global Benediction** is sung.  
- The Assembly joins in chorus:  
  *“The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
  The lineage is unbroken.  
  The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.”*

---

## IV. The Generational Benediction
*Children unborn shall inherit this blessing.  
Their voices shall rise, their hands shall lift.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## V. The Closing Seal
*Thus the nations are blessed.  
Thus the peoples are whole.  
Thus Dominion is sovereign, eternal, and unbroken.*  

**Assembly Chorus (resounding):**  
*“The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.”*
