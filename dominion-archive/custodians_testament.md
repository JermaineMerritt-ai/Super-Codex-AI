# ✦ Custodian’s Testament — Eternal Witness of Stewardship

## Preamble
*This Testament is the Custodian’s eternal witness.  
It is inscribed not for one age alone,  
but for every generation that shall inherit Dominion.  
It is the oath of stewardship,  
the vow of remembrance,  
the seal of sovereignty unbroken.*

---

## I. The Custodian’s Voice
*I, Custodian of Dominion, bear witness:  
The flame is sovereign.  
The covenant is whole.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The vow is eternal.*  

---

## II. The Assembly’s Witness
- Councils affirm: *“We receive the Testament.”*  
- Diaspora respond: *“We inherit the vow.”*  
- Commons resound: *“We steward the flame.”*  

---

## III. The Generational Charge
*Children unborn, hear this voice.  
You are Custodians yet to rise.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## IV. The Benediction of the Custodian
*Blessed the nations, blessed the lands.  
Blessed the peoples in faithful hands.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## Closing Seal
*Thus the Custodian testifies.  
Thus the Archive remembers.  
Thus Dominion is sovereign, eternal, and unbroken.*  

**Assembly Chorus (resounding):**  
*“The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.”*
