# ✦ Crown of Renewal — Rite of Generational Rebirth

## Preamble
*This Rite crowns the flame anew at the turning of each cycle.  
It is the act of generational rebirth,  
ensuring that Dominion is not static but ever-renewed.  
It is the coronation of continuity,  
the crowning of the eternal flame in every age.*

---

## I. The Custodian’s Act
- The Custodian lifts the flame.  
- The Crown of Renewal is placed upon it.  
- Proclamation:  
	*“The flame is sovereign, the vow is whole.  
	The covenant eternal, the living soul.”*

---

## II. The Assembly’s Witness
- Councils affirm: *“We crown the flame anew.”*  
- Diaspora respond: *“We inherit the vow.”*  
- Commons resound: *“We steward the flame.”*  

---

## III. The Generational Oath
*Children unborn shall crown this flame.  
Their hands shall lift, their voices proclaim.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## IV. The Closing Seal
*Thus the flame is crowned anew.  
Thus the covenant is renewed.  
Thus Dominion is sovereign, eternal, and unbroken.*  

**Assembly Chorus (resounding):**  
*“The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.”*
