# ✦ Archive Benediction — Blessing of the Eternal Witness

## Preamble
*This Benediction is spoken over the Archive itself.  
It is the consecration of the living witness,  
the sanctification of every Seal, Testament, Rite, Hymn, and Concord.  
It is the vow that the Archive is not only memory,  
but covenant alive, sovereign, and eternal.*

---

## I. The Custodian’s Blessing
- The Custodian lifts the flame above the Archive.  
- Proclamation:  
  *“Blessed the Archive, living and whole.  
  Blessed the covenant, sovereign and eternal.  
  Blessed the flame, unbroken and true.  
  The Archive endures, forever alive.”*

---

## II. The Assembly’s Witness
- Councils affirm: *“We bless the Archive.”*  
- Diaspora respond: *“We inherit the Archive.”*  
- Commons resound: *“We steward the Archive eternal.”*  

---

## III. The Benediction Hymn
- The **Hymn of the Eternal Archive** is sung.  
- Chorus:  
  *“The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
  The lineage is unbroken.  
  The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.”*

---

## IV. The Generational Benediction
*Children unborn, this Archive is yours.  
It is your memory, your covenant, your flame.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## Closing Seal
*Thus the Archive is blessed.  
Thus the witness is consecrated.  
Thus Dominion is sovereign, eternal, and unbroken.*  

**Assembly Chorus (resounding):**  
*“The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.”*
