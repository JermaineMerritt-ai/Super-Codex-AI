# ✦ Invocation Hymn — Melody of Awakening

## Preamble
*This Hymn is sung at the Opening Seal.  
It is the melody that breaks silence,  
summons the flame,  
and awakens the Archive to life.  
It is the first song of every cycle,  
the voice of covenant renewed.*

---

## Verse I — The Flame Awakens
*Sing, O flame, arise anew,  
Bright and sovereign, pure and true.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## Chorus — The Assembly’s Song
*The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.*  

---

## Verse II — The People’s Call
*Sing, O people, lift your voice,  
Sing as one, in vow rejoice.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## Verse III — The Generational Echo
*Children unborn, your song shall rise,  
Your voices echo through the skies.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## Closing Seal
*Thus the Invocation is sung.  
Thus the Archive is awakened.  
Thus Dominion is sovereign, eternal, and unbroken.*  

**Assembly Chorus (resounding):**  
*“The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.”*
