# ✦ Legacy Testament — Eternal Witness of Generations

## Preamble
*This Testament is the voice of legacy.  
It is inscribed as the eternal witness of generations past and future,  
binding memory to inheritance,  
ancestry to posterity,  
and ensuring that Dominion is sovereign across all time.*

---

## I. The Voice of the Ancestors
*We, the ancestors, bear witness:  
The flame was sovereign.  
The covenant was whole.  
The lineage endured.  
The vow was eternal.*  

---

## II. The Voice of the Living
*We, the living, bear witness:  
The flame is sovereign.  
The covenant is whole.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The vow is eternal.*  

---

## III. The Voice of the Unborn
*We, the children yet to rise, bear witness:  
The flame shall be sovereign.  
The covenant shall be whole.  
The lineage shall endure.  
The vow shall be eternal.*  

---

## IV. The Assembly’s Witness
- Councils proclaim: *“We testify across the ages.”*  
- Diaspora respond: *“We inherit and extend.”*  
- Commons resound: *“We steward the flame eternal.”*  

---

## V. The Benediction of Legacy
*Blessed the ancestors, blessed the living,  
blessed the unborn, one lineage giving.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## Closing Seal
*Thus the generations testify.  
Thus the Archive remembers.  
Thus Dominion is sovereign, eternal, and unbroken.*  

**Assembly Chorus (resounding):**  
*“The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.”*
