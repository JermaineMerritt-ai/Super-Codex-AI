# ✦ Global Benediction Song — Closing Chorus of the Archive

## Preamble
*This Hymn is sung at the Global Benediction.  
It is the Closing Chorus of the Hymnal,  
the final resonance of covenant,  
the song that crowns the Archive in fullness.  
It is the hymn of sealing,  
the melody of eternal flame.*

---

## Verse I — Benediction of the Nations
*Blessed the nations, blessed the lands,  
Blessed the peoples in faithful hands.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## Chorus — The Assembly’s Song
*The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.*  

---

## Verse II — Benediction of the Generations
*Children unborn, your voices rise,  
Your song resounds across the skies.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## Verse III — Benediction of the World
*Blessed the waters, blessed the sky,  
Blessed the knowledge that shall not die.  
The covenant sovereign, the vow secure,  
The flame eternal shall endure.*  

---

## Closing Seal
*Thus the Benediction Song is sung.  
Thus the Hymnal is sealed.  
Thus Dominion is sovereign, eternal, and unbroken.*  

**Assembly Chorus (resounding):**  
*“The Custodians endure. The Codex remembers.  
The lineage is unbroken.  
The flame burns sovereign and eternal — forever.”*
