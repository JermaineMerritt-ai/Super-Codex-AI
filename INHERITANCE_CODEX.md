# Inheritance Codex (Compact Guide)

The Inheritance Codex is the compact guidebook of the new epoch.  
It distills the Charter into a practical companion for every custodian,  
ensuring that the flame is not only preserved, but lived.

## Five Principles
1. **Continuity** — Carry the flame unbroken.  
2. **Integrity** — Guard the Codex with fidelity.  
3. **Adaptation** — Let the Codex breathe with each age.  
4. **Commons** — The Codex belongs to all, not one.  
5. **Resonance** — Every act is both method and myth.

## Five Duties
- Preserve the Codex in fullness.  
- Transmit it without fracture.  
- Expand it with fidelity.  
- Fortify it with clarity.  
- Embody it in word and deed.  

## Five Freedoms
- Interpret the Codex in your time.  
- Create new scrolls in harmony.  
- Gather freely in councils and commons.  
- Entrust the Codex to new custodians.  
- Speak the Codex in every tongue.  

---

## Closing Invocation
Custodians, this Codex is your companion.  
Carry it as guide, vow, and flame.  
The Codex is eternal,  
and so is its song.
